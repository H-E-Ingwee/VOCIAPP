VOCI CURRICULUM

Three Pillars of Transformation

Our curriculum is built on three foundational pillars. The VOCI Intake is now open for you to join this transformative journey.

Pillar 1: Effective Communication

Master the art of public speaking, storytelling, and persuasive pitching to inspire and influence others with unparalleled clarity and impact. This pillar builds confidence and hones your ability to connect with any audience.

  • Foundations of Communication
  • Mastering Public Speaking
  • Storytelling for Impact
  • Idea Pitching & Persuasion
  • Advanced Communication
  • Cross-Cultural Communication

Pillar 2: Personal Branding

Equip yourself to build authentic, value-driven identities that stand out, attract opportunities, and amplify your impact in a competitive world. This pillar focuses on crafting your unique professional narrative.

  • Foundations of Personal Branding
  • Your Unique Value Proposition
  • Crafting Your Brand Identity
  • Amplifying Your Brand Online
  • Networking & Relationship Building
  • Pitching Yourself for Opportunities

Pillar 3: Principled Leadership

Raise a generation of leaders grounded in strong ethics, resilience, and an unwavering commitment to societal transformation. This pillar equips you to lead with integrity and make a lasting positive impact.

  • Foundations of Leadership
  • Ethical Decision-Making
  • Team Management & Delegation
  • Resilience & Adaptability
  • Leading for Societal Good
  • Collaboration & Partnerships
OUR FOCUS

The 8 Spheres of Influence

We empower leaders to apply their skills in the specific areas they are called to transform. Find your sphere and start your journey.

Government

Seeking out those with a heart for public service, law, or policy to lead with integrity and advocate for justice.

Economics

Identifying entrepreneurs and business leaders called to ethical practices and wealth creation.

Science & Technology

Connecting with innovators and thinkers to articulate their vision and bring creative, ethical solutions to complex problems.

Church

Partnering with those in ministry, theology, and spiritual leadership to deliver messages with clarity and impact.

Family

Equipping parents, counsellors, and advocates to build strong family units and mentor the next generation.

Education

Engaging with teachers, students, and administrators to inspire learning and shape educational systems.

Communication

Nurturing journalists, artists, marketers, and PR professionals to be truth-tellers and storytellers.

Arts & Entertainment

Seeking out artists, musicians, and performers to use their creative gifts to inspire hope and transform culture.
