âš ï¸ CONTINGENCY PLANS

If Attendance Drops Below Target

  • Investigate root causes through member surveys
  • Offer flexible participation options (recorded sessions, hub-only engagement)
  • Increase mentorship touchpoints and support
  • Revisit session timing/location if applicable
  • Create re-engagement campaign with incentives/recognition

If Members Consistently Miss Assignments

  • Reassess assignment complexity and time requirements
  • Provide assignment writing support/office hours
  • Extend deadlines for struggling members
  • Simplify initial assignments to build confidence
  • Create assignment buddy system for peer support

If Peer Mentoring Relationships Struggle

  • Provide additional mentor training and resources
  • Offer mentor coaching sessions
  • Facilitate mentee-mentor matching adjustments if needed
  • Create structured mentorship conversation guides
  • Establish more frequent check-ins and accountability

If Content Creation Falls Behind

  • Share the responsibility across more team members
  • Pre-plan and batch content creation
  • Simplify content formats (text + photo vs. complex videos)
  • Invite members to contribute guest content
  • Use content templates to speed up creation process
