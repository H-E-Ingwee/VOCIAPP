OUR JOURNEY

The Story Behind VOCI's Transformative Impact

From a bold vision to a global movement, discover how VOCI is becoming a catalyst for change, empowering voices to influence meaningful impact in the society.

"In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God... We believe words carry the power to create, enlighten, and lead."

― Inspired by John 1:1, 5 & Genesis 1:3

Founded in 2025 at Murang’a University of Technology, The Voice of Change Initiative (VOCI) emerged from a profound vision: to have a platform that can help people grow in their oratory skills. We had the passion for seeing people communicate and express themselves boldly and in clarity.We sat down a number of times trying to see where could we begin from. At first in 2024 we wanted to come up with a club and call it "The Great Orators Club". We were determined to see it running back then but for some reasons we were never able to make it happen. With time Brian Ingwee, the founder, continued to have the burden to help people in their communication skills but he had not found the best way to do so. It is until when they started having deep conversations with Hopewin Adams that a new fire was re-inginted. VOCI came as a vision so bold and clear on the 12th of March 2025. Ingwee first shared the concept with Hopewin and that is when the journey of re-writing the vision of VOCI began. Coming up together with the founding team the jounery of VOCI officially kicked off with a renewed spirit and a more clear strategy of bringing the vision to life. We are now no longer just interested in growing in public speaking but raising and building a network of transformative leaders in the major spheres of life. We continue to expand our reach, believing that by equipping one leader, we create a ripple effect that transforms many.

OUR CORE BELIEFS

Our Vision & Mission: Guiding Our Purpose

These statements encapsulate the very essence of VOCI, defining our aspirations and our commitment to empowering transformative leaders globally.

Our Vision

To empower individuals, especially leaders, in identifying their full potential and sphere of influence and equipping them with exceptional communication skills, authentic personal branding, and principled leadership skills enabling them to create meaningful impact across all the spheres of life.

Our Mission

VOCI is dedicated to empowering students, emerging leaders, and society with the skills, values, and confidence to communicate effectively, build authentic personal brands, and lead with excellence. Through transformative programs, mentorship, and meaningful community engagement, we aim to develop a network of change-makers who deliver undeniable impact in every sphere of influence.

OUR PRINCIPLES

The Core Values Guiding Our Movement

Our values are not just words; they are the bedrock of our identity, inspiring every action and interaction within the VOCI community.

Integrity

Upholding honesty and authenticity in all actions and communications.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in communication and leadership development.

Empowerment

Unlocking individual potentials for personal, professional, and societal growth.

Impact

Driving measurable and positive change in society and beyond through action.

Collaboration

Building a supportive and collaborative network of diverse leaders and partners.

Creativity

Equipping individuals with skills to communicate creatively and craft innovative solutions.
