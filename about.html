OUR JOURNEY

The Story Behind VOCI

From a bold vision to a growing movement, discover how VOCI is becoming a catalyst for change.

Founded in 2025 at Murang'a University of Technology, The Voice of Change Initiative (VOCI) emerged from a profound vision: to have a platform that can help people grow in their oratory skills.

We had the passion for seeing people communicate and express themselves boldly and in clarity.We sat down a number of times trying to see where could we begin from. At first in 2024 we wanted to come up with a club and call it "The Great Orators Club". We were determined to see it running back then but for some reasons we were never able to make it happen. With time Brian Ingwee, the founder, continued to have the burden to help people in their communication skills but he had not found the best way to do so. It is until when they started having deep conversations with Hopewin Adams that a new fire was re-ignited. VOCI came as a vision so bold and clear on the 12th of March 2025. Ingwee first shared the concept with Hopewin and that is when the journey of re-writing the vision of VOCI began. Coming up together with the founding team the journey of VOCI officially kicked off with a renewed spirit and a more clear strategy of bringing the vision to life. We are now no longer just interested in growing in public speaking but raising and building a network of transformative leaders in the major spheres of life. We continue to expand our reach, believing that by equipping one leader, we create a ripple effect that transforms many.

OUR CORE BELIEFS

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To empower individuals, especially leaders, in identifying their full potential and sphere of influence and equipping them with exceptional communication skills, authentic personal branding, and principled leadership skills enabling them to create meaningful impact across all the spheres of life.

Our Mission

VOCI is dedicated to empowering students, emerging leaders, and society with the skills, values, and confidence to communicate effectively, build authentic personal brands, and lead with excellence. Through transformative programs, mentorship, and meaningful community engagement, we aim to develop a network of change-makers who deliver undeniable impact in every sphere of influence.

OUR FOCUS

The 8 Spheres of Influence

We empower leaders to apply their skills in the specific areas they are called to transform. We aim to identify and nurture leaders in these key spheres to ensure VOCI's influence extends beyond our immediate network and creates a lasting, multi-generational ripple effect in society.

Government

Seeking those with a heart for public service, law, or policy to lead with integrity and advocate for justice.

Economics

Identifying entrepreneurs and business leaders called to ethical practices and wealth creation.

Science & Technology

Connecting with innovators and thinkers in research and technology to bring creative, ethical solutions.

Church

Partnering with those in ministry, theology, and spiritual leadership to deliver messages with clarity and impact.

Family

Equipping parents, counsellors, and community advocates to build strong family units and mentor the next generation.

Education

Engaging with teachers, students, and administrators to inspire learning and shape educational systems.

Communication

Nurturing journalists, artists, marketers, and PR professionals to be truth-tellers and storytellers.

Arts & Entertainment

Seeking out artists, musicians, and performers to use their creative gifts to inspire hope and transform culture.
